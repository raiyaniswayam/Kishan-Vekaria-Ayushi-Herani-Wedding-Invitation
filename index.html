Frequently Asked

Where Can I Find the Wedding Registry?

+
Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift we could ask for. We are not registered for physical gifts and kindly request no boxed gifts at the celebration.

Is There a Dress Code?

+
Sangeet / Ring Ceremony: Cocktail or Indian festive attire is encouraged. Think vibrant colors, glam details, and outfits perfect for an evening full of dancing and celebration. Guests are welcome to wear traditional Indian attire, fusion wear, or cocktail attire.

Wedding Ceremony: Indian Formal Attire / Black Tie Optional. Traditional Indian attire such as sarees, lehengas, sherwanis, and kurtas are warmly encouraged.

Is the Venue Accessible by Ride-Share Services Like Uber?

+
Yes, the venue is accessible via ride-share services during the day and night.

Does the Venue Have Parking?

+
Yes, complimentary parking will be available onsite.

What Kind of Food Will There Be at the Wedding?

+
A thoughtfully curated selection of traditional and fusion Indian cuisine will be served, along with non-alcoholic drinks.

What Time Is the Wedding?

+
Please refer to the "Schedule" section above for detailed event timings. The Sangeet is on Friday, July 17 (5:30 PM – 11:00 PM) and the Wedding is on Sunday, July 19 (8:00 AM – 3:00 PM).
